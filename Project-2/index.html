where the art of dance comes alive through the mesmerizing expressions of Kathak and the graceful movements of Bharatnatyam.

We Teach

Kathak

Journey through the mesmerizing rhythms and storytelling artistry of Kathak, a classical dance form that traces its roots to northern India. At Natarajan Dance Academy, we bring this age-old tradition to life, teaching the intricate footwork, expressive abhinaya, and dynamic spins that make Kathak a captivating dance form.

Bharatnatyam

Immerse yourself in the grace and beauty of Bharatnatyam, a classical dance form with its origins in the southern part of India. Natarajan Dance Academy is your gateway to this timeless art, where we teach the elegant movements, intricate hand gestures, and emotive expressions that define the essence of Bharatnatyam.

Odissi

Explore the rich heritage of Odissi, a classical dance form rooted in the culture of Odisha. At Natarajan Dance Academy, we offer you the opportunity to discover the sculptural poses, fluid movements, and mythological narratives that make Odissi a unique and captivating dance tradition, all within a nurturing and inspiring learning environment.
